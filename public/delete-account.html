Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy. If you wish to permanently delete your SeamStrike account and all associated data (including rosters, schedules, and strategies), please choose one of the methods below.

Option 1: Delete via App (Instant)

1. Open the SeamStrike app.
2. Go to Account in the main menu.
3. Scroll to the bottom "Danger Zone".
4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Option 2: Web Request

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access it, please click the button below to email our support team. We will process your deletion request within 48 hours.

Request Deletion via Email
Warning: Account deletion is permanent. All data associated with your user ID will be wiped from our servers and cannot be recovered.
